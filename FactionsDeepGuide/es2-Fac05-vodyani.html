Parasites in silk robes. The Vodyani are a faction defined by a single, radical design premise: your empire is not a territory, it is a fleet of arks that happens to land occasionally. Righteous Fury: War Without War

The diplomatic inversion that makes Vodyani aggression so difficult to counter.
âš¡ The Religious Law That Rewrites the Rules of Engagement MECHANIC â–¾
WHAT RIGHTEOUS FURY DOES
"Righteous Fury" is the base Religious law, available from turn 1. Its effects in practice:
  • You can operate your fleets inside opponent-controlled influence zones without declaring war
  • You can attack enemy ships and invade enemy systems without declaring war
  • You can cross closed borders as if they don't exist
  • Opponents cannot force a truce unless they first declare war on you â€” which costs them Influence
The practical result: you are always the technical aggressor in terms of action, but always the technical neutral party in terms of diplomatic status. Opponents who want to stop you must formally declare war, spending their Influence to do so.
THE DIPLOMATIC INVERSION â€” WHY DEFENDERS LOSE
In a normal ES2 aggression scenario: the attacker pays Influence to declare war, is subject to forced truces, and faces the Diplomatic penalty of being the aggressor. All three costs fall on the attacker.

With Righteous Fury: the defender pays Influence to declare war (in order to gain the right to force a truce). The defender is now the declared aggressor. The Vodyani accumulate Influence faster than the apparent upkeep cost (law asymmetry) and can refuse forced truces essentially for free. The defender who closes their borders has also reciprocally locked themselves out of Vodyani space â€” which they may need to access for trade or diplomacy. The entire cost structure of ES2 aggression is reversed.
BRAINWASHING MINOR FACTIONS â€” THE VASSAL SHORTCUT
Once the Vodyani reach full Approval with a minor faction, they can "brainwash" it into a vassal state. This provides continuous FIDSI and Essence bonuses, grants the Vodyani access to that minor faction's trait (the only way they can obtain minor faction traits), and locks the minor faction from enemy assimilation via Influence or Dust â€” they can only be liberated by ground invasion. This is the sole mechanism for Vodyani to access minor faction traits and is worth prioritizing for the highest-value minor faction in your neighborhood.
